# Scoundrel
A terminal based game based on the card game Scoundrel.

## Rules for Playing
+ 44 Card Deck
    + Spades ranked 2-14
    + Clubs ranked 2-14
    + Hearts ranked 2-10
    + Diamonds ranked 2-10
+ Spades and Clubs are monster cards and deal damage equal to their rank (Ace High).
+ Hearts are potion cards and heal HP equal to their rank.
+ Diamonds are weapon cards and reduce damage by their rank.
+ You start with 20 max HP and no weapon.
+ Combat:
    + If you do not have a weapon, you take full damage from a targeted monster.
    + If you have a weapon, subtract weapon damage from the monsterâ€™s value. Once you defeat a monster, you may only attack other monsters with a value less than to the previously slain monster. If slain in this way you will not take damage.
    + If your weapon cannot defeat a monster on the floor, you will fight the monster barehanded.
+ Fleeing: You may flee a room if it is too challenging, takes 3 turns to recharge.

# Please leave feedback and have fun playing.
Take your time, its a strategy game not an action roguelite.
